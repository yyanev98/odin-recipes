Moussaka

Description

This is a traditional Bulgarian recipe which very popular in the country till this day.

There is also a greek version of the recipe which is made with eggplants instead of patatoes

Ingridiets

  • 2 lb (~1 kg) potatoes, cut in small cubes
  • 1 lb (1/2 kg) ground meat
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon crushed black pepper
